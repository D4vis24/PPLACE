Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases in the app. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Your premium access will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone’s Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find PPL Ace, and tap Cancel Subscription. You’ll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Can I switch from monthly to lifetime?

Yes! Cancel your monthly subscription first, then purchase the lifetime option from the upgrade screen. The lifetime purchase is a one-time payment with no recurring charges.

How many questions are in the question bank?

PPL Ace covers all major FAA Private Pilot knowledge areas. The question count is shown in Settings → Question Bank. We regularly update and expand the question bank.

Will my progress be saved if I delete the app?

All study data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your progress will be permanently removed. We recommend completing your study goals before removing the app.

Is PPL Ace a replacement for ground school?

No. PPL Ace is a supplemental study tool designed to help you practice for the FAA written knowledge test. It should be used alongside official FAA training materials and instructor-led ground school.
